1. Website purpose

This website provides a personal checklist to help you track which furniture items you own in the game Palia. Your checklist is stored locally in your browser (for example via Local Storage).

2. Data storage & privacy

  • No account required: the website does not require registration or login.
  • Local-only storage: your checklist data is saved on your device in your browser storage. It is not uploaded to a server by this website.
  • Clearing your browser data (cookies/site data) may remove your saved checklist.

Tip: If you switch device or browser, your checklist will not automatically follow you.

3. Cookies & analytics

This website does not intentionally set tracking cookies. If third-party services are added in the future (e.g., analytics, error reporting), this page will be updated.
